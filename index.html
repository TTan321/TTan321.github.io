Hi there, I am a medical biller turned software engineer. I graduated from San Jose State University with a bachelors in Economics. After a couple of years a medical biller, I felt like I was not growing and was doing unfilling tasks and I also hated being placed on long holds by the various insurance companies. During the pandemic I started to study Python on my own by reading and practicing the problem exercises from a python textbook I bought on amazon. I learned about coding bootcamps from some friends and heard the great experiences they had from attending them. I decided to apply to App Academy, a 24 week coding bootcamp that taught full stack engineering. At App Academy, I was able to hone my skills as a software engineer and pick up new languages (JavaScript, ReactJs, SQL) and frameworks (Express, Sequelize, Flask, SQLAlchemy) to be a full stack software engineer where I was able to create full stack web applications.
